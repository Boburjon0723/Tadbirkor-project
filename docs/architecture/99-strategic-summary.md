```text
1. Landing + 30 kunlik trial
2. Ro‘yxatdan o‘tish
3. Savollar orqali tizimni moslashtirish
4. Role-based interface + ombor scope (SALES/WAREHOUSE)
5. Product + Variant
6. Basic Warehouse (ko‘p nuqta = ko‘p ombor)
7. B2B Order
8. Product Mapping
9. Dispatch / Goods Receipt
10. Debt Ledger
11. POS (kassa, mijoz, naqd/nasiya, POS hisoboti)

Chek printer, narx PIN, smena va karta gateway keyingi bosqich. Asosiy kuch — B2B oqim + chakana (alohida mijoz/qarz modeli) + modulga mos dinamik UI.
```
